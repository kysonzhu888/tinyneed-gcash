Privacy

GCash Fee Calculator runs in your browser. It does not collect your GCash account, phone number, password, OTP, or transaction history.

The tool may use standard server logs, browser storage for basic preferences, and Google AdSense to show ads. Google may use cookies or similar technologies for ad measurement and personalization depending on your settings.

This website also uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to measure aggregate site traffic and performance. We do not configure it to send calculator inputs, account details, phone numbers, transaction history, or other custom identifiers. Cloudflare may process request and browser analytics data under its privacy policy.

Do not enter private account details into this calculator. Use the official GCash app or GCash support channels for real account issues.
