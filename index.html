Fee rules used in this calculator

Offline cash in

First PHP 8,000 per calendar month is free. The amount above that limit is charged 2%.

Bank transfer

GCash lists PHP 10 per bank transfer, effective July 4, 2026, with a PHP 50,000 max per transaction.

Cash out

The entered cash-out amount is what you receive. GCash deducts that amount plus the PHP 18 RCBC fee or the estimated 2% partner fee from your wallet.

PayPal / Payoneer

PayPal cash in is calculated at 1%. Payoneer is listed as free.

Send money

GCash lists no fee for the first 500 sends and receives per month, then PHP 5 for select power users.

Buy load

Globe and Smart have small fixed fees by load amount. DITO is listed as free.
